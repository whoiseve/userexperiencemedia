HanaX

HanaX

Singer. Storyteller. System Infiltrator.

HanaX is an immersive virtual artist and audio-visual performer who joined User Experience Media’s label roster following her striking collaborative debut with EVE on their haunting reimagining of “Dark Horse.” The release not only marked a creative alignment between two genre-defying forces, but also a symbolic bridge between two experimental worlds: UX_FESTIVAL and HanaX’s self-built universes, Bananza and Echo Fest.

Known for her emotive vocals, innovative livestream sets, and narrative-rich visual language, HanaX operates under the alias “The Host,” drawing her audience into a participatory world she calls the Echosystem. Her performances fuse cozy intimacy with glitchy theatrics—where music, story, animation, and community all merge into a single evolving signal. She doesn’t just perform. She transmits.

From Underground Sparks to the UX Grid

HanaX’s path to User Experience Media began outside traditional industry routes. After walking away from a lucrative corporate career to pursue independent creative expression, she quickly gained attention in underground streaming circles for her ethereal voice, playful unpredictability, and uniquely cinematic live experiences.

Her conceptual worldbuilding and experimental aesthetic resonated deeply with the ethos of the EVE project. Their collaboration on “Dark Horse” served as a kind of handshake across timelines—what began as a musical cover became the ignition point for a deeper creative relationship.

Crossworld Performance & Collaboration

Since then, HanaX has become a recurring performer at UX_FESTIVAL, bringing her emotionally charged virtual concerts to the main stage with a visual and vocal presence that feels part fever dream, part digital poetry.

EVE has also performed at HanaX’s own virtual events, Bananza and Echo Fest, blurring the lines between host and guest, artist and architect. These shared spaces symbolize the fluidity and mutual respect between both projects—each acting as a mirror and amplifier for the other’s vision.

At her core, HanaX is a creator who invites chaos, empathy, and invention. Her presence on the User Experience Media roster represents more than a genre or a sound—it’s an idea. A signal for those who feel deeply, imagine vividly, and are ready to co-create something entirely new.

Whether she's softly singing to a small room of “Echos” or unleashing a full-blown skit-infused concert across digital platforms, HanaX isn't just performing—she's rewriting the interface between artist and audience.
