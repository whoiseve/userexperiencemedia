About User Experience Media

User Experience Media is a future-facing creative collective that began as a response to the limitations of traditional live events, launching immersive virtual festivals that connected audiences across continents. Born out of a passion for pushing boundaries through digital art, music, and storytelling, our initial goal was to create a space where artists and fans could gather without borders. A vision realized through interactive, live-streamed experiences hosted in virtual reality and across streaming platforms like Twitch and YouTube.

What started as a single experiment has grown into a full-fledged multimedia ecosystem. Today, UX Media operates as a hybrid label, publisher, and artist management platform, supporting a diverse roster of creators in electronic music and beyond. We work closely with artists not only to release music, but to develop long-term careers, facilitate licensing opportunities, and explore innovative forms of content distribution. Our in-house creative team leads production for audiovisual releases, marketing campaigns, and short-form digital media designed to resonate across platforms.

ROSTER



Alongside our label and festival work, we’ve also expanded into content creation through exclusive behind-the-scenes interviews and original programming. Our flagship series, TIMECODE, serves as a conversation hub for artists, industry professionals, and forward thinkers, diving deep into the stories, philosophies, and experiences that shape creative culture. Whether it’s through virtual stages, curated playlists, or insightful dialogues, our mission remains the same: to create meaningful experiences that celebrate artistry, amplify underrepresented voices, and reimagine what a music media platform can be in the digital age.

From immersive festivals to holistic artist support, User Experience Media continues to grow with one core idea at heart, that connection, creativity, and community should always come first.
