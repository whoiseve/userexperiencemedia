ORITY

itsmeority

itsmeority is an anonymous ambient and atmospheric music project known for its emotive, genre-blurring soundscapes.

Choosing to remain faceless, itsmeority invites listeners to engage purely with emotion and atmosphere—free from image, identity, or persona. The music exists as a quiet refuge, a space where mood outweighs spectacle, and subtlety speaks louder than words.

Since its quiet emergence in the early to mid-2020s, itsmeority has wandered through sonic territory ranging from ambient and lo-fi to gentle liquid drum & bass, cinematic downtempo, acoustic electronica, and soft-edged house. Whether creating melancholic piano sketches, misty vocal textures, or warm analog-inspired grooves, itsmeority crafts music meant for introspection, late-night drives, or moments of stillness.

Label & Milestones

In 2024, itsmeority became one of the first few artists to release under User Experience Media’s Label Division, joining a growing roster dedicated to genreless innovation and storytelling through sound. Their debut release on the label, a relaxed, atmospheric "wave inspired" song called "crystalline memories" with weaving ambient textures and soft vocal fragments.

This release marked a defining moment in the project's evolution, signaling a deeper commitment to long-form storytelling through audio and an invitation to future collaborators across visual and sonic disciplines.

ority isn’t just about creating songs... it’s about building moods. It’s a vehicle for exploring emotional resonance through sound design, ambient texture, minimalism, and human vulnerability. The project refrains from loudness, favoring silence, subtlety, and nuance as a form of resistance in an overstimulated world.
